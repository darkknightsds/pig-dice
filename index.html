How to Play:

  • - The goal of the game is to be the first player to reach 100 points.
  • - If you roll a 1, you score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • - If you roll any other number, it is added to your turn total and your turn continues.
  • - If you choose to pass, your turn total is added to your game score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
